At SRE Universitas Hasanuddin, our mission is to be at the forefront of the Indonesian energy transition led by students

Empowering Youth Leadership

Empowering Youth Leadership

Cultivating a generation of passionate and skilled young leaders by providing platforms for learning, collaboration, and hands-on experiences that empower youth to contribute actively to the country's energy transformation.

Knowledge Dissemination and Skill Development

Serving as a knowledge hub by providing publications, organizing events, and offering training courses focused on renewable energy technologies. We aim to equip our members with the latest insights and practical skills necessary to address the challenges of the evolving energy landscape.
