Our Journey in Snippet
Writer: Sitti Afifah S. Halada

Internal Event

Internal Event is a series of activities exclusively for SRE Unhas members. This activity aims to monitor and evaluate performance, provide capacity building training for members, and build closeness among members to create a positive working environment.

Re-Visit

Learning firsthand about renewable energy, especially how it works will be very effective if visiting the site directly. SRE Unhas organised Re-Visit as an initiative where members visit an RE Power Plant to gain firsthand knowledge of its operations. During this activity, participants will learn about the functioning of wind turbines, the process of converting wind energy into electricity, and the challenges and opportunities in the development of renewable energy in Indonesia.

(Source: Documentation of SRE Universitas Hasanuddin)

Re-Connect

Re-Connect is a board bonding program carried out to increase the sense of closeness and kinship within the internal scope of SRE Unhas. This activity is expected to increase the sense of family and sense of organisational ownership in each member of SRE Unhas.

(Source: Documentation of SRE Universitas Hasanuddin)

HC Clinic

HC Clinic is a work program that aims to monitor the performance of each member through a personal approach, namely O2O. It is hoped that this work program can increase the work comfort of management and help follow up on things that are considered to be hampering management's performance.

(Source: Documentation of SRE Universitas Hasanuddin)

ESCALATE

Escalate is a mentoring and capacity building program for Hasanuddin University SRE members which is carried out through integrated soft skills and hard skills training based on the needs of the Management.

(Source: Documentation of SRE Universitas Hasanuddin)
