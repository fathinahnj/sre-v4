Event Collaboration

I-REIF

In an effort to promote and support the development of Renewable Energy in Indonesia, SRE Unhas had the opportunity to collaborate with HIRE Idn and the German Agency for International Cooperation (GIZ) in organizing the I-REIF batch of PLTB which was held in Makassar on February 7 2024. I-REIF became an event for exchanging experiences and inspiration in implementing the development of new, renewable energy in Indonesia.

(Source: Documentation of SRE Universitas Hasanuddin)

Kalla Youth Fest

Apattasa by KYF is an activity initiated by the Makassar City Government, Kalla Group and SRE Unhas to overcome environmental damage, especially to river flows in Makassar City. Sre Unhas contributed by making a tool in the form of a trash barrier which is useful for dispelling and collecting rubbish flowing in rivers, making it easier for cleaning staff to clean up rubbish in rivers.

(Source: Documentation of SRE Universitas Hasanuddin)

Indonesia Youth Summit 2024

As collaborators in the Indonesia Youth Summit (IYS 2024), SRE UNHAS (Society of Renewable Energy Universitas Hasanuddin) is committed to empowering youth to tackle challenges in sustainable energy and climate change. Through our involvement in the youth assembly and panel discussions, we aim to contribute innovative ideas and practical solutions, helping to drive the realization of the "Indonesia Emas 2045" vision and promote national energy independence and sustainable development.

(Source: Documentation of SRE Universitas Hasanuddin)
