Going Places, Green Style:

Eco-Friendly Tour with TEMAN Bus Makassar

Writer: Shofiyyah
Editor: Afifah

Traffic congestion is a major issue in many large cities in Indonesia, including Makassar. The rapid increase in private vehicle ownership has led to overcrowded streets and declining mobility for residents. One essential step to address this congestion is providing a public transportation system that is comfortable, safe, and reliable.

To tackle these challenges, Indonesia's Ministry of Transportation introduced TEMAN Bus (Transportasi Ekonomis Mudah Aman dan Nyaman), a Bus Rapid Transit (BRT) system designed to reduce private vehicle usage and encourage people to switch to public transit.

What is TEMAN Bus?

With the name Trans Mamminasata, TEMAN Bus was first launched in Makassar at the end of 2021 and now operates along five main routes in the city, including at Hasanuddin University.

TEMAN Bus is part of the Buy The Service (BTS) program developed by the Directorate General of Land Transportation at the Ministry of Transportation. This program is designed to streamline road-based mass transit services in urban areas, incorporating cashless payment technology to enhance passenger safety and convenience. TEMAN Bus adheres to government-set service standards, with the primary goal of offering the public an affordable and high-quality transportation option.

Why should we take TEMAN Bus?

TEMAN Bus is an ideal option for people who do not own private vehicles but need inexpensive and comfortable transportation for daily activities. The buses are equipped with facilities to support passenger comfort, including comfortable seating, a priority area for those in need, and air conditioning. The cashless payment technology makes the payment process more efficient, allowing for faster and safer travel.

In Makassar, TEMAN Bus operates two types of buses: medium-sized buses with a capacity of 40 passengers (20 seats) and large buses accommodating up to 60 passengers (30 seats). Each bus includes a priority area for passengers who need it, along with technology-based services to support accessibility and comfort.

TEMAN Bus offers highly affordable fares, especially for students. Student passengers pay only Rp2,000 per trip by showing their Student ID card (KTM). For the general public, the fare is Rp4,600 per trip. These fares are subsidized by the government to ensure that public transportation remains accessible to all members of society.

A Perfect Choice for Eco-Friendly Transportation!

TEMAN Bus serves as an eco-friendly public transportation initiative that aims to make urban travel more sustainable and efficient. By promoting an accessible and reliable bus service, TEMAN Bus encourages residents to opt for public transit over private vehicles, addressing both traffic congestion and environmental concerns. This shift not only helps alleviate the overcrowding of city roads but also reduces the carbon emissions and air pollution that result from excessive private car usage.
