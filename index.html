About SRE UNHAS

The Society of Renewable Energy (SRE) is a non-profit professional association of more than 100 universities and companies. SRE is the premier source for technical knowledge providing publications, events training courses and online resources. SRE Universitas Hasanuddin was established in 2020, which is part of SRE Indonesia, which has a vision to accelerate the Indonesian energy transition through inclusive youth empowerment and multi-institutional collaboration

October Edition: Green Economy

Green economy is a system of economy that aims to improve human well-being and social equity, while significantly reducing environmental risks. This concept emphasizes sustainable economic growth, where economic activities do not damage the environment or excessively deplete natural resources. A green economy seeks to balance economic needs with environmental sustainability for future generations.
